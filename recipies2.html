Broccoli Casserole

Create a comforting casserole that works for weeknights as well as holiday

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound broccoli, cut into pieces
  • 1 (10.75 oz.) can cream of mushroom soup
  • 2 large eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded Cheddar
  • 1 stick (1/4 lb.) salted butter, cut into pieces
  • Pepper, as much as you see fit
  • 1 sleeve Ritz crackers, crushed (1/3 of a 12 oz. box)

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Spray a 9-by-13-inch baking dish with cooking spray
  3. Steam broccoli until crisp-tender, 7 minutes.
  4. Mix soup, eggs, mayonnaise, cheese, butter and pepper into a large bowl
  5. Drain broccoli; spread evenly into the sprayed baking dish
  6. Add the other ingredients evenly on top of the broccoli
  7. Sprinkle with Ritz crackers
  8. Bake for 30 minutes
